Participants:
We collected data from 130 participants. Participants' ages were 18-40 (M = 27.10, SD = 5.50), 43 (33.08%) identified as female. Participants were recruited using Prolific. Data was collected remotely using Pavlovia. Participants received Â£7.5 (GBP) as an hourly rate, in addition to an optional bonus (see below). 


Data Screening:
Note that data from a participant or a specific trial can be invalid due to more than one reason. We removed task trials where responses on the Comparison stage were incorrect (13.29% of all responses), trials where the response time on any of the three stages was longer than 10 Seconds (1.54%). Then, we removed trials where the total response time was slower than 99.50% or faster than 0.50% of the trials, calculated individually for each participant (1.39% of all data). Next we removed the data of 2 (1.54%) participants which had less than 50% of valid responses, either due to slow responding, or low accuracy. In total, we removed 16.28% of the data, either in single trials or whole participants.
